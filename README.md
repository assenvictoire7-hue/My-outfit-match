This is a website that I designed to help people match outfits
 and improve their fashion choices based on style, age, and 
 occasion. The platform also provides information about the 
 different clothing markets in Cameroon, helping users discover 
 where they can find fashionable and affordable clothes, 
 especially in markets located in Yaoundé.
One of the key features of the website is its focus on fashion
 guidance across different age groups. It presents various ways 
 of styling dresses and outfits for people of different ages,
  
 including children, adolescents, and adults. By recognizing
  that fashion preferences and dressing styles vary according to 
  age, the website provides personalized outfit ideas that are 
  suitable, stylish, and culturally appropriate.

Additionally, the website offers step-by-step guidance on how individuals can dress based on their age category. These steps help users understand what clothing styles, color combinations, and outfit pairings may best suit them. The goal is to make fashion more accessible and easier to understand, especially for people who may struggle with matching clothes or choosing outfits.
Another important aspect of the website is its promotion of 
Cameroon’s local fashion markets. It highlights different 
markets in Yaoundé where users can purchase clothes, fabrics,
 and fashion accessories. This helps support local businesses 
 while making it easier for users to identify affordable 
 shopping locations.
